Masquerader

2023

This costume was put together for an event for Lafayette College sophomores called "Midway Masquerade". The original headdress idea was going to be a large-scale fabrication, but I ended up using a less-complicated one that I had used for other costumes in the past.

The skirt, top, tights, boots, and accessories were sourced, but the train and mask I made myself. The mask is a simple plastic mask covered in several layers of tulle, and the train is a large piece of fabric with small weights hand-sewn into the corners to keep it from flying around too much in the wind.

Doll Necromancer

2023

One of my more intricate costumes, majority of the pieces for the "Doll Necromancer" were custom-made. The gloves and belt I crocheted and the pants I hand-sewed. The tunic, made of scrap fabric I machine-sewed together, was made using a pattern that I created myself. The fur hood I also machine-sewed, but it was several years before as an unrelated project.

Nox

2024

One of the clubs on the the Lafayette College campus has a murder mystery party every year, and this was the costume I wore to play the murderer. Each group is assigned a color, and I requested to be in the "blue" group (fairies) so that I could make costume pieces to reuse for my Renaissance Faire costume later in the year. Both the tunic and cowl I machine-sewed using patterns I found online, while the rest of the items in the costume were sourced.

2024

This costume was originally made to attend the PA Renaissance Faire in October 2024. I also made a necklace which matched with another one I made for my friend. All other items were sourced.
